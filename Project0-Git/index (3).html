Favourite Achievement

I am very proud of many of my achievements in my short career so far but the one that stands out the most to me was the 2019 SIABS where I had the privilege of representing my country for the first time. I competed in the 100m and I was able to place a respectable 2nd against stiff competiton from England,Wales and Scotland.It was also a very good learning experience for me as I learned how to prepare for competitions abroad and staying in different surroundings than I'm usually accustomed to.I definitely hope to compete in more international competitions in the future.
