Why Did I Choose Athletics?

I first began athletics in school competing in the school competitions that took place at the end of the school year.I often placed very well in these competitions and it is a very a crucuial reason why I decided to take up the sport.My PE teacher was very supportive of me and highly encouraged me to continue athletics into my adolescence which was highly motivating.

I started out at my local club at the age of 11 and enjoyed it thoroughly.I had the opportunity to meet new people however I had a passion for football at the time and that was my main priority.In 2017,after a few years of playing football, I decided to return to athletics as I felt it was where my talents were at and I have been doing it ever since.
